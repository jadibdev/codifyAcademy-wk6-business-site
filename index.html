About

Amber Perez

Owner & Professional Dog Walker

Hi There :-)

My name is Amber Perez, owner of Bay City Paws and Professional Dog Walker. I've grown up and spent my whole life around dogs. I have more then 5 years of professional experience working with dogs. In addition to dog walking, I've worked at doggy daycares, kennels, a veterinarian clinic.

Before creating my own dog walking business, I worked for a large dog walking company in San Francisco. I grew up with lots of dogs in my family's home, and that's how my love for dogs started. Since I was little, I was interested in going to school to be a Veterinarian.

On the other hand, after working at a local vet clinic in San Mateo I had a change of heart and felt that it wasn't something for me. I wanted to do something with dogs where I could be outdoors and having fun. I wanted to see the happy side of dogs, not the sick and injured one.

So, Dog walking was my true calling card. I presently own 4 rescue pups that I love so much! I aspire one day to open a rescue place of my own for dogs, and create a brand that will create funds to donate to animal shelters.

Approach

My goal here is to provide your dog or doglet with lots of daily exercise. Dogs need exercise everyday for their happiness and well-being. I do early and afternoon walks for dogs. All doggie walks are off-leash (unless otherwise requested by owner) With so many abundant parks and open spaces in this city, your pup will never be bored.

I do daily hikes and trail walks around the Presidio or take them on outings to Crissy Field, so they can run and play fetch. The quality of care and attention provided is the same they would receive at home. Your four legged companion will go home so knackered, I give you my tired-dog guarantee!

I personally see to it that every one of my dogs will get along in the pack and are socialised as part of my on-boarding process. I want your pup to thrive and be happy and safe with the family pack. I provide pick-up and drop-off texts as a courtesy so you know where your pup is through out the day.

I update my gallery on my website and my facebook daily with pictures of your dogs whilst they're oot 'n aboot.

Policy

I don't presently have any breed restrictions. I do, however, reserve the right-during any part of the on-boarding or interview process-to reject a dog deemed unstable or dangeous. I have a responsiblity to this community and my pack.
